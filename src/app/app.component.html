WORDLE

Wordle is a daily word-guessing game that has taken the internet by storm. Here's the breakdown of how it works:

Goal: Guess the secret five-letter word.

How to play:

  1. Guess a five-letter word and hit enter.
  2. The letters in your guess will change color to give you feedback.
  3. Use the feedback to guess another five-letter word.
  4. Keep guessing until you guess the correct word.

Feedback background colors:

  • Green indicates that the letter is in the correct spot.
  • Yellow indicates that the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • White indicates that the letter is not in the word.
  • The player has innumerous tries to guess the word.
